Pasta with Red Lentil Sauce and Salad

Real Easy Weekday

Ingredients

Pasta

Pasta sauce

1-2 Tbs. olive oil / 15-30 mL

8 oz. mushrooms, sliced / 225 g (optional, could add any vegetable or none at all) (roasted veggies)

1-2 Tbs. avocado oil / 15-30 mL

1/3 cup dry red lentils / 80 mL (about 1 cup cooked)

Nutritional yeast (optional)

Directions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Add olive oil after straining.
2. Boil red lentils until soft, approximately 15 minutes. Strain.
3. In a medium pan, saute mushrooms in avocado oil until browned lightly.
4. Add lentils and pasta sauce to pan with the mushrooms.
5. Check to see if there are any raw veggies that need to be used that could
be added to the salad (tomatoes shown here).
6. Serve pasta with sauce and a side salad. Top with cheese if using.
